Beef Burger


Ingredients
1 large egg
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground black pepper
1 pound ground beef
½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
Steps:

1.Prepare the Meat: Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions. Shape each portion into a patty about 3/4 inch thick. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty to prevent it from puffing up during cooking.
2.Season the Patties: Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
3.Cook the Burgers: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 3 to 4 minutes per side, or until they reach the desired doneness (usually 160°F for medium).
4.Toast the Buns: While the patties are cooking, toast the hamburger buns in a separate skillet or on the grill until golden brown.
5.Assemble the Burgers: Place each cooked patty on the bottom half of a toasted bun. Add any desired toppings like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, ketchup, mustard, etc.
6.Serve: Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot!
