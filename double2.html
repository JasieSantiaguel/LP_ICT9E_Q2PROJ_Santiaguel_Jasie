The Ultimate Smash Cheeseburger


Ingredients
1 lb (450g) of 80/20 ground beef
salt
ground black pepper
4 slices of American cheese
Steps:

1. Prepare the beef by dividing 1 lb of ground beef into 4 equal portions and forming them into loose balls.
2. Preheat the skillet by heating a cast-iron skillet or griddle over high heat until smoking hot.
3. Smash the patties by placing the beef balls in the skillet and immediately smashing them down with a spatula or burger press to form thin patties.
4. Cook the patties for 2-3 minutes per side, allowing the edges to crisp up. Add a slice of cheese to each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
5. Toast the buns in the skillet until golden brown.
6. Assemble the burger by placing the cheese-topped patties on the bottom buns and adding your preferred toppings like pickles, onions, or condiments.
7. Serve by topping with the other half of the bun and serving immediately.
