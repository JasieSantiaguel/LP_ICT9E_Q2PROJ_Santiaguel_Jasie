Homemade Hamburgers


Ingredients
1 lb ground beef
salt
Black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
4 hamburger buns
Steps:

1. Prepare the patties by mixing 1 lb ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Shape the mixture into 4 equal patties, making a slight indentation in the center of each.
2. Cook the patties in a skillet or on a grill over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes per side until they reach your desired doneness (160Â°F for well-done).
3. Toast the buns in the skillet or on the grill until golden brown.
4. Assemble the burgers by placing each cooked patty on the bottom bun, then adding toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, ketchup, mustard, or pickles.
5. Serve by topping with the other half of the bun and serving immediately.
