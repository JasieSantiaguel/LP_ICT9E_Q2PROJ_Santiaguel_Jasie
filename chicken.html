Ground Chicken Burgers


Ingredients
1 lb ground chicken
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
1 egg
1 garlic clove
2 tbsp chopped parsley
Salt
Pepper
Burger buns
Steps:

1. Start by mixing 1 lb of ground chicken with 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs, 1 egg, 1 minced garlic clove, 2 tablespoons of chopped parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix the ingredients until everything is well combined.
2. Once the mixture is ready, shape it into 4 equal-sized patties.
3. Heat a skillet or non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it. Cook the patties for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) and are fully cooked.
4. While the patties are cooking, toast the burger buns in the skillet or oven until they are golden brown.
5. Once the patties are done, place them on the bottom half of each toasted bun. Add your desired toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments.
6. Finally, top the burgers with the other half of the bun and serve immediately. Enjoy your homemade chicken burgers!
