The Ultimate Double Cheeseburger


Ingredients
1 lb (450g) ground beef (80% lean)
salt
ground black pepper
4 slices of cheddar cheese
4 hamburger buns
Steps:

1. Prepare the Patties: Mix together ground beef, salt, pepper, and any other seasonings. Divide the beef into 8 equal portions, then shape each into a patty about 1/4 inch thick. Make the patties slightly larger than the buns, as they will shrink during cooking.
2. Cook the Patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the first set of patties for 2-3 minutes on one side, then flip them. Add a slice of cheese to each patty while they cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until fully cooked and the cheese is melted.
3. Toast the Buns: While the patties are cooking, toast the hamburger buns in a separate skillet or under a broiler until golden brown.
4. Assemble the Burger: Start by placing a slice of cheese on the bottom half of each bun. Add a cooked patty, then stack another patty on top. Add any desired toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, onions, ketchup, or mustard.
5. Serve: Top the burger with the top half of the bun and serve immediately.
