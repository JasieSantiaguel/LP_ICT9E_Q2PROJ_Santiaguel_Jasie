Crispy Fried Chicken Burger


Ingredients
2 chicken thighs
2 chicken thighs
1 cup buttermilk
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
Steps:

1. Prepare the chicken by mixing 1 cup buttermilk with spices like paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add chicken thighs (or breasts) to the buttermilk mixture and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for best results.
2. Coat the chicken by combining 1 1/2 cups of flour with spices (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper). Dredge the marinated chicken in the flour mixture, pressing lightly to coat it evenly.
3. Fry the chicken by heating oil in a deep fryer or skillet to 350°F (175°C). Fry the chicken in batches for about 5-7 minutes until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should be 165°F or 75°C).
4. Toast the buns in a skillet or oven until golden brown.
5. Assemble the burger by placing the crispy chicken on the bottom half of each toasted bun. Add toppings like lettuce, tomato, pickles, and mayo or any desired condiments.
6. Serve by topping with the other half of the bun and serving immediately.
